Dox Treatment and Reprogramming

The Yamanaka factors are proved to be able to able to rejuvenate the old mice and improve their cognitive abilities. I want to see whether the change of transcriptomics can be seen in the process of rejuvenation.

Using RNA-seq method with the methylation data, I found there is a large discrepency among the same group. It suggests we need to exclude some outliers in the group. After exluding some outliers, we identified some DEGs and they are proven to be related to some neuron development process.
